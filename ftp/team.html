Chef Alejandro Rivera

The Genius Behind Sogbu

Chef Alejandro Rivera is the visionary force behind Sogbu’s acclaimed culinary success. With a reputation that spans continents, Alejandro is known for his masterful ability to blend tradition with innovation, creating dishes that are both visually stunning and a feast for the senses. His meticulous attention to detail and passion for sourcing the finest ingredients have earned him not only three Michelin stars but also the admiration of critics and food enthusiasts alike.

Having trained in some of the world’s most prestigious kitchens, Chef Rivera’s philosophy is rooted in respect for the ingredients, the art of storytelling through food, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Under his leadership, Sogbu has become a destination for those seeking an extraordinary culinary experience. Chef Rivera’s commitment to excellence, paired with his creative brilliance, continues to shape the future of fine dining, solidifying his place as one of the most respected chefs in the world.

Sous-Chef Mateo Vargas

The Heart of Sogbu’s Kitchen

Sous-Chef Mateo Vargas is the talented force behind the flawless execution and creativity in Sogbu’s kitchen. Known for his precision and passion, Mateo has become a rising star in the culinary world, playing a key role in the restaurant’s journey to three Michelin stars. His ability to bring Chef Alejandro Rivera’s visionary concepts to life with his own innovative touch has made him an indispensable part of the team.

With a background that spans prestigious kitchens across Europe and Latin America, Sous-Chef Vargas is renowned for his mastery of technique and flavor balance. His commitment to refining every dish while respecting the essence of each ingredient elevates the dining experience at Sogbu. Mateo’s dedication to excellence and his relentless drive to push the boundaries of modern cuisine make him a vital force in the restaurant’s continued success.

The Sogbu Team

Masters of Culinary Art

At the core of Sogbu’s three Michelin-star success is a dedicated team of chefs, sommeliers, and staff who share a passion for perfection. Led by Chef Alejandro Rivera and Sous-Chef Mateo Vargas, each member brings their own expertise, creativity, and commitment to the art of fine dining. Together, they create a seamless dining experience, where every dish is executed with precision and every detail is attended to with care. This exceptional team is the driving force behind Sogbu’s reputation as one of the finest culinary destinations in the world.
